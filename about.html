About

I was a Marine. During Operation Iraqi Freedom I led an infantry squad on missions that ran from the oil fields of southern Iraq to the streets of Baghdad. The years since took me through a long string of jobs and two careers before I started writing.

I've written two novels, Durango Sky and Selected, a Western and a political thriller. Between books I write essays on music and books at rhythmandink.com.

I live in Tulsa, Oklahoma. For what I'm working on right now, see my Now page.
