About

J. Allen Wolfrum is a fiction writer and former Marine. During Operation Iraqi Freedom he led an infantry squad on missions that ran from the oil fields of southern Iraq to the streets of Baghdad.

Since then he's taken the long way around: press operator in a plastics factory, warehouse stocker, restless college student, CPA, restless graduate student, management consultant, data scientist — and, finally, writer. (He's still working on the guitar.)

He lives in Tulsa, Oklahoma, where he writes fiction, a few lines of poetry, and keeps a running log of what he's up to on his Now page.
