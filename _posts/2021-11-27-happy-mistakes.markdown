## Happy Mistakes  

On a warm fall day  
I stepped off onto the trail  
Just north of Santa Fe  
The smell of high-desert pine on every inhale  

A 3 mile loop, no need to rush  
Only 3,000 feet of elevation gain  
A cactus in bloom and a nose full of sagebrush  
The cars down below feel so inhumane  

A fork in the trail, left or right?  
Frantic search. Phone. Map. On the front seat of the car  
Too steep, the peak is out of sight  
I flipped a coin and confidently marched on, au revoir  

Turned out to be longer, harder, and more beautiful ... I call it a shortcut  
A happy mistake, preferable to being a robot.
