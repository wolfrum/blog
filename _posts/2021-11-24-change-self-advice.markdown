## Change (Advice to myself)
 
It's inevitable. "That's a fact, Jack."  
Try growing without it.  
Try to stop it by sitting in front of the TV with a six-pack  
End up angry and alone, that ain't no comedy skit   

Adapt and overcome. Roll with the punches. Earn some grit.  
That's what they say.  
It all boils down to two words, "don't quit."  
That's the order of the day, every day.  

No need to overthink it, just keep plugging away.
