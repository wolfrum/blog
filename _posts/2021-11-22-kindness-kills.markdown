## Kindness Kills
 
I don't know why I keep doing it   
We both know that's a lie  
I should put my foot down and quit  
I should, I could, I'm gonna...my misguided battle-cry  

Those words are even easier than turning a blind-eye  
No work required. Say them out loud and skip along your merry way  
Months pass. It comes back, grinning, in a suit and windsor-tie  
"You just want to play? I shouldn't. Maybe if we don't get too carried away."  

"On second thought, no thanks. I'm just gonna walk away."
