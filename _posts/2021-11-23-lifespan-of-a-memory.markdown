## The Lifespan of a Memory
 
Memories exist outside of our mind  
They live in every object we see  
Take a look around. What did you find?  
A time when you were carefree?  

Or maybe a partner softly touching your knee?  
What you found is both new and old at the same time  
How can that be?  
Memories don't fade. They leave and come back to visit at storytime  

That's how they last a lifetime
